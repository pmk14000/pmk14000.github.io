About Me

I am a Nigerian creative writer who is passionate about sharing insightful pieces, scripting comics and writing stories to be told on television. While I understand the appeal of a happy ending, I prefer plot twists and the thrill of unpredictability. My writing draws on a love of superhero stories and the intricate narratives of pro wrestling. That said, I always prioritize my clients' vision.

If you’re looking for scripts that surprise, provoke, and entertain, I’d love to collaborate.
