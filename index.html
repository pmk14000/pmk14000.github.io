About Me

My name is Michael Kolawole Williams and I have been cursed with the ability of top tier storytelling. I am a scriptwriter who's impassioned about comic books. I have followed a wide variety of thrilling materials from superheroes to regular action, since childhood, and have now eased into the life of creating similar characters and storylines for a living. I have been contracted by Comic Republic and have interfaced with some of their IP, writing for them for a short period. I believe that good storylines and quality characters are at the core of every comic book that would leave an impact in the heart of a reader, and this is my daily drive.
